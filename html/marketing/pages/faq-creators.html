Creator FAQ

Common questions about becoming a creator on OnlyPaws.

What am I subscribing to as a creator?

The creator subscription gives you access to the OnlyPaws creator platform, allowing you to monetize your pet content through subscriptions.

Are there any content guidelines I should be aware of?

Yes. OnlyPaws is designed for respectful pet-related content. Content should prioritize animal well-being, safety, and responsible ownership.

Creators are expected to publish original content they own or have the rights to use. Fake, misleading, or stolen content is not permitted and may result in content removal or account suspension.

What content can I post on OnlyPaws?

Allowed content includes photos and videos of pets, daily routines, training moments, grooming, playtime, educational tips, and creator updates related to pet care and lifestyle.

What content is not allowed?

Content that involves animal abuse or harm, neglect, promotion of violence, illegal activities, hate or harassment, graphic injuries, animal fighting, exploitation, or unsafe behavior is strictly prohibited.

Sexualized, suggestive, or fetish-oriented content involving animals is not permitted.

Is OnlyPaws a hosting or social network?

OnlyPaws is a subscription-based platform focused on pet creators. It is not a general social network.

How do I get paid?

Payments are handled securely through Stripe. Earnings are collected via subscriptions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account dashboard.

Go to Dashboard → Subscription / Settings → Cancel subscription. No emails or support tickets are required.

What happens after I cancel?

When you cancel, your subscription will not renew and you will not be charged again. Your access will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Will I get a refund if I cancel?

No. Payments already made are non-refundable.

Can I reactivate my subscription?

Yes. You can reactivate your subscription at any time.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. There is no minimum commitment and no cancellation fee.

Does canceling delete my account?

No. Canceling a subscription only stops future billing. Your account and content remain available.

Is my content protected?

OnlyPaws is designed to respect creator content, but creators remain responsible for what they publish.

Who can become a creator?

Anyone producing original pet-related content and complying with platform guidelines.
