Fan FAQ

Everything you need to know as a fan on OnlyPaws.

What is OnlyPaws?

OnlyPaws is a subscription platform where fans can support pet creators and access exclusive content.

How do fan subscriptions work?

Fans subscribe to individual creators and get access to subscriber-only content. Billing is handled securely by Stripe.

How do I cancel a subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account dashboard.

Go to Dashboard → Subscription / Settings → Cancel subscription. No emails or support tickets are required.

What happens after I cancel?

When you cancel, your subscription will not renew and you will not be charged again. Your access will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

After the billing period ends, the subscription will automatically expire.

Will I get a refund if I cancel?

No. Payments already made are non-refundable. You will continue to have full access until the end of the current billing period.

Can I reactivate my subscription after canceling?

Yes. You can reactivate your subscription at any time before the end of the current billing period from your dashboard.

If the subscription has already expired, you can subscribe again at any time.

Is there a minimum commitment or cancellation fee?

No. There is no minimum commitment and no cancellation fee. You are free to cancel whenever you want.

Does canceling delete my account or content?

No. Canceling a subscription only stops future billing. Your account and content remain available according to your account type.

What kind of content will I see?

Content varies by creator and may include photos, videos, and behind-the-scenes moments.

Is OnlyPaws safe?

Payments are processed by Stripe, and the platform follows standard digital service practices.

Who do I contact for support?

For any issues or questions, contact us at onlypawssupp@gmail.com.
